## Design Philosophy for Future Reconstruction

**Eastland Mall is NOT:**
- A Bradbury (ornate, Victorian, decorative)
- A traditional suburban mall
- A heritage architecture showcase

**Eastland Mall IS:**
- A TWA-terminal-style concourse
- A space station with a parking lot
- KKT 80's vision of the future
- Functional > ornamental
- Scale > intimacy
- Light > enclosure
- Postmodern pastiche of transportation architecture applied to retail

**This is a TRANSPORT HUB that calls itself a MALL.**

The white lattice towers, tensile fabric roof, teal/blue color system, and massive scale all reinforce the "departures lounge" aesthetic. The design language prioritizes movement, flow, verticality, and natural light through engineered fabric systems.

**Reconstruction Goal:** Capture the liminal quality of a transit space frozen in amber, a concourse to nowhere, a waiting room for a future that never arrived.
