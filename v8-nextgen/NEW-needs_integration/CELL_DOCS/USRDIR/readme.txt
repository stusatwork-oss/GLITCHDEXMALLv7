


Does only support a display resolution of 720p

[Gamepad Controls]
	Wait and press <START> to start the demo

	<SELECT> 				Advances to the next demo mode
	<START> 				Recenters camera
	<SELECT+START> 			Restarts demo (must press and hold SELECT, then press START)
	<L1+L2>					Toggle debug HUD
	<R1+R2>					Save input data
	<SELECT+START+L2+R2> 	Exits demo


[Mode (Controls)]
	1. Single Duck
		Left stick moves duck, x jumps
	2. Boats far
	3. Boats near
	4. 6 ducks
	5. 100 ducks
	6. 200 ducks
	7. Big duck
	   For modes 2-7:
		L1/R1 fire cannonballs
		Analog sticks move boats in direction (but ships cannot go backward!)
		R2 enables Mode 1 duck controls for duck #1


[Camera Controls]
	L2+square/circle: 	Camera X
	L2+triangle/cross: 	Camera Y
	L1+square/circle: 	Camera orbit
	L1+triangle/cross: 	Camera zoom
