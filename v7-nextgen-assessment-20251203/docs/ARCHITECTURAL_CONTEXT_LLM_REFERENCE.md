## The "Reactor Containment Zone" Food Court

**The food court is not a dining area. It is industrial theater.**

3. **Arrival point:** You face **the black open mouth** - the theater entrance
4. **The bowl:** Food court wraps around theater as peripheral space

#### The Theater as Focal Void
**CRITICAL GEOMETRIC INSIGHT:** The 6-screen underground theater entrance is not "adjacent to" the food court.

**The theater entrance IS the gravitational center of the food court bowl.**

- The escalators deliver you **directly facing the theater entrance**
- The "blank black open mouth" visible at the bottom of the descent = theater entrance
- Food vendors arranged in perimeter around this central void
- Axial composition: Upper level → Descent → Theater void

**Why "reactor containment zone"?**
- Descent into technological/industrial space

- "Am I being too conservative with my estimates?"

### Confidence Level Assignment

**HIGH confidence requires:**
- Countable elements (steps, cables, tiers, blocks)
- Standard code dimensions (door heights, railing heights)
- Direct measurement with clear scale reference
- Multiple photo confirmation

**MEDIUM confidence accepts:**
- Single photo measurement with perspective correction
- Estimated scale reference (block size, vendor booth)
- Reasonable architectural assumptions

**LOW confidence is for:**
- Visual estimates without scale reference
- Perspective-compressed distances
- Inferred from typical construction practice

**When in doubt, assign MEDIUM not LOW.** This was ambitious architecture - err toward larger, not smaller.
